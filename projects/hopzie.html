Problem Solving

Challenges & Solutions

Navigating the hurdles of scaling an AI commerce engine while maintaining trust.

01
The Challenge

Pivot Under Legal Constraint

Original service direction was halted due to regulatory limitations while Google funding was already secured, requiring immediate strategic repositioning.

Action & Outcome

Action: Identified expansion signals in the YouTube creator economy and hypothesized demand for structured creator-commerce infrastructure.

Execution: Conducted rapid interviews (5 creators & viewers) in an emerging market with limited desk research data.

Outcome: Designed and launched MVP within constrained runway.

02
The Challenge

Scale Commitment Decision

After MVP validation, needed to determine whether to scale within a rapidly platform-consolidating commerce ecosystem.

Strategic Insight

Action: Initiated independent discussion with Google platform representative to assess long-term viability amid expanding native shopping infrastructure.

Insight: Identified structural limitations for third-party aggregation layers as platform-native storefront modules accelerated.

Decision: Strategically archived before committing additional resources.

Retrospective

What I Learned

Learned that building products is not only about launch, but about reading market signals and knowing when to stop.

Efficiency Driven

Principle 01

Validated user intent through rapid interviews and MVP launch, minimizing overengineering before structural risk assessment.

Applied Result

Quick interviews across layer-1 viewers & creators (Sep 2023) → MVP launch timeline focusing on core intent (Nov 2023)

Communication Architect

Principle 02

Translated platform constraints into internal alignment, clarifying where automation adds value versus fixed infrastructure.

Applied Result

Defined standardized commerce UI components to reduce coordination cost; balanced AI auto-curation vs regional manual control.

Inquiry-Driven Detection

Principle 03

Used external platform signals to decide when to stop—before sunk-cost drift intensified by competitive pressure.

Applied Result

Detected platform-native consolidation signals early; made critical pivot decision before competitive decompression.
